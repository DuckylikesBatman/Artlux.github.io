ArtLux Return Policy

Our commitment to excellence extends to every aspect of your art collecting experience

Original Artworks

Each original piece is carefully inspected before shipment. If your artwork arrives damaged, please contact us within 7 days of delivery with photographs of both the artwork and packaging.

We will arrange for return shipping and either repair, replace, or refund your purchase.

Commissioned Works

Due to the custom nature of commissioned pieces, all sales are final once production begins (typically 3-5 days after order confirmation).

You may cancel within 24 hours of placing your order for a full refund. After this period, a 15% design fee will apply to cancellations.

Return Process

  1. Contact us at returns@artlux.com with your order details
  2. Include clear photographs of any damage
  3. We'll provide prepaid shipping instructions
  4. Upon receipt, we'll process your refund within 5 business days

Refund Methods

Refunds are issued to the original payment method. Processing times vary:

  • Credit cards: 3-5 business days
  • PayPal: 24-48 hours
  • Bank transfer: 5-10 business days

Special Circumstances

For international returns, customers are responsible for any customs duties or taxes associated with the return shipment.

Special order items and framed pieces may require additional handling time for returns.

Artwork Care

To preserve your artwork's value:

  • Keep away from direct sunlight
  • Maintain stable humidity levels
  • Handle with clean hands or cotton gloves
