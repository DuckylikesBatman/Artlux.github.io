Artistry in Every Stroke

Founded by Manal Yehya, ArtLux transforms spaces through masterful decorative painting and timeless artistic finishes.

The ArtLux Vision

With over 30 years of international experience across the Middle East and Europe, Manal Yehya established ArtLux in 2019 to redefine decorative artistry.

Our work blends traditional craftsmanship with contemporary design, creating spaces that tell stories through texture, color, and meticulous detail.
